  1. Strength Training (30-45 minutes)

    • Upper body (e.g., push-ups, dumbbell presses, rows)
    • Lower body (e.g., squats, lunges, deadlifts)
    • Core and flexibility (e.g., planks, Russian twists, yoga)

  2. Cardio Workout (20-30 minutes)

    • Options: running, cycling, swimming, or a high-intensity interval training (HIIT) session.
    • Adjust intensity based on your fitness level and goals.

  3. Meditation Practice (10-15 minutes)

    • Guided Meditation: Use a meditation app or audio guide that provides instructions and support.
    • Mindfulness Meditation: Focus on your breath, sensations, or a specific object. When your mind wanders, gently bring your focus back to the present.
    • Loving-Kindness Meditation: Focus on sending positive thoughts and well-wishes to yourself and others.
